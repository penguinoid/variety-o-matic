Variation-o-Matic

Variety 1 PAF

Easy to recognize. The only circular printed variety. Common on older reels. The only variety on 'Gold foil' reel styles. There is a small difference between 2 circular printed varieties. This is the early variety with 'PAT. APPLIED FOR'. Common

Variety 1 P

Easy to recognize. The only circular printed variety. Common on older reels. The only variety on 'Gold foil' reel styles. There is a small difference between 2 circular printed varieties. This is the later variety with 'PATENTED' beneath the center hole. Common

Variety 2

'Sawyers' in italics left from center. 'REEL NO.' printed above reel number on top. Three lines right from center. Fairly common

Variety 3

'Sawyer's' in normal print left from center. 'REEL NO' printed above reel number on top. Four lines right from center. Common

Variety 4

'Sawyer's' in normal print left from center. 'NO.' printed left from reel number. Reel number within the reel title on top right. Scarce

Variety 5

'SAWYER'S PORTLAND, ORE.' on top of center. Variety issued by the U.S. government. Rare Varieties 6,7 and 8 are similar.

Variety 9

Two lines left of the center. Number in the middle on top of center.

Variety 10

Three lines left of the center. Second line not indented reads 'PORTLAND, OREGON' Line three indented. Rare

Variety 11

Three lines left of the center. Line two and three indented. 'NO.' before reel number in the middle on top. Less common

Variety 12

Three lines left of the center. Line two and three indented. Similar to variety 11 but no 'NO.' preceding the reel number. Fairly common

Variety 13

Similar to variety 12. '© Sawyer's' and copyright year at bottom First variety with a copyright year. Less common

Variety 14

Three lines left of the center. All three are left aligned. Common

Variety 15

Similar to variety 14 with '© Sawyers' bottom Scarce

Variety 16

Similar to variety 14 with '© Sawyer's' and copyright year in script at bottom Scarce

Variety 17

Similar to variety 14 with '© Sawyer's Inc.' and copyright year in script at bottom Scarce

Variety 18

Similar to variety 12 Three lines left of the center. Line two and three indented. On first line 'Sawyer's Inc.' Scarce

Variety 19

Three lines left of the center. On first line 'Sawyer's Inc.' Line two and three indented. First typeset variety. Common

Variety 20

Similar to variety 19 Copyright year at bottom Less common

Variety 21

Similar to variety 19 'Sawyer's, Inc.' at bottom Less common

Variety 21a

Similar to variety 19 'Sawyer's, Inc.' and copyright year at bottom Rare

Variety 22

Similar to variety 19 'Sawyer's, Inc.' and below that 'Portland 7,Oregon' at bottom Scarce
